This hotel is an excellent place to stay.  The hotel is in a very convenient location, close to downtown and the "L".  Every morning a lovely continental breakfast is served on each floor, with a wide variety of pastries and the like.  The rooms are comfortable, large, modern, and inviting.  The bar has excellent hors d'oevres.  If there is anything you need the staff will help you out and do so in a friendly and professional manner.
