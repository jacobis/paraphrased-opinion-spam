The Intercontinental, located on Michigan Avenue is a beautiful hotel on the Magnificent Mile that offers many amenities for the price.  Modestly priced rooms (around 75-90 bucks/night) that are extremely clean and luxurious.  Within walking distance is the NBC, Tribune, and Sun-Times buildings.  The service is excellent!
