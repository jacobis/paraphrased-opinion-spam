The Palmer Hotel is an impressive place for a vacation get-away spot. Many who stay here are able to get a discount through their rewards program or some other offer. For many, the hotel has proven to be well worth it. The suites are spacious and clean, staff members are friendly, and despite not having a fancy view, the hotel is still located in walking distance to many spots for sight seeing and eating. If you are looking for a well maintained hotel that has a lot to offer in terms of space, service, and excellent room features, Palmer Hotel would be a great choice.
