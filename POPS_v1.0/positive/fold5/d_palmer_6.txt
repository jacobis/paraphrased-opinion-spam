Palmer House in downtown Chicago is an excellent choice of hotel for your stay in town. The location is perfect if you want to experience Chicago's culture and there are many locations within walking distance for dining and entertainment including the Art Institute and Millennium park just a couple of blocks away.  The hotel lobby is gorgeous with old world furniture mixed with modern conveniences that give a comfortable but historic feel to the place.  The rooms follow this trend and have beautiful woodwork furniture and very comfortable bedding with plenty of pillows for lounging as well as excellent amenities.  The staff is efficient and friendly and will definitely make you feel welcome for the duration of your stay.
