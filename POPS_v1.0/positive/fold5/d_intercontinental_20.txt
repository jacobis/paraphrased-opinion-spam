The Intercontinental on Michigan Avenue is a lovely place to stay. Located super centrally, within a hop, skip and a jump (or short bus ride) from countless top class restaurants and attractions, they have an ultra-friendly and competent staff whose highest priority is serving the needs of their guests. The rooms a just plain beautiful, with modern decor and appointed very nicely. To top it all off, the affordable rates make the Intercontinental a no-brainer choice if staying in the downtown area.
