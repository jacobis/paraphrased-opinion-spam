This hotel is conveniently located one block from public transportation and local entertainment venues.  It's large suites are perfect for families or extended visits. Friendly staff make your stay complete by adding thoughtful touches such as the morning papers at your door each morning and music in your room on your arrival home from shopping or sight seeing in the evening.  The hotel has a modern and funky decor which along with the morning coffee and evening wine tastings may make you feel like you are living larger than your wallet suggests.
