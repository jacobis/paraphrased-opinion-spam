This is the best hotel in  downtown Chicago.  Great prices for large, spacious rooms with the most comfortable beds ever.  Lot of unexpected pleasures here -- free continental breakfast on EVERY floor, featuring fresh coffee, pastries and juice; evening receptions with free cocktails and appetizers, and great jazz CDs in the room.  Easy walking access to the Miracle Mile and all the bars and restaurants.  Friendly and helpful staff.  Why stay anywhere else?
