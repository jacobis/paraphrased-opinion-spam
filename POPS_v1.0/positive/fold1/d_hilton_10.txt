The Chicago Hilton first and foremost can be booked at a very affordable rate.  Considering the high quality of this hotel, the price is most definitely a steal!  Once you get to your room, you will find a large room with two bathrooms.  There is even a king size bed.  It is all high luxurious and extremely relaxing.  The view outside the windows is wonderful as well.  You will can see Grant Park from your room.  It is in walking distance and is very nice to walk through and enjoy during your stay. Also, there a some more attractions just within a mile of the hotel  such as the Shedd Aquarium and Soldier Field.  Lastly, if you are wanting a great place to enjoy a meal you will be able to find it at this hotel.  The food is reasonably priced and very good.
