A stay at the James hotel in Chicago is bound to be a memorable experience. From its prime location, a two-minute walk from the shops on Michigan Avenue, to David Burke's Primehouse restaurant, the James has all the elements to be the right hotel for your trip. Rooms are spacious and well-appointed, featuring the most comfortable beds this side of heaven. But the best feature of the hotel is its service. Our friendly, helpful Concierge, hotel managers and room service have one goal in mind: your comfort and satisfaction. Even your pet will be welcome, with ice water and doggie treats available at the front door.
