The Ambassador East is a charming and classic old-fashioned hotel.  The service is top-notch, and the staff is always eager to make a guest's stay memorable.  Their concierge can arrange for almost anything that a guest might request.  The hotel is located attractions and nightlife like the magnificent mile and Rush Street.  A favorite place to go is the famous Pump Room Restaurant, where Frank Sinatra used to be a frequent patron.  The Ambassador East is a great place for anyone to stay when visiting Chicago.
