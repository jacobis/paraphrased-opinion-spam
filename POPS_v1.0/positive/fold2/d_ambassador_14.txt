The Ambassador East Hotel is a beautiful old hotel with a classic design, located conveniently near many of  Chicago's north side attractions. Staying there makes touring the sites easy as many are within walking distance. The rooms are clean and quiet. The staff are friendly and courteous and will to go the extra mile to ensure your stay is comfortable and relaxing, and will help you with any of your needs. While you stay there you can visit the adjoining The Pump Room, a restaurant known for it's famous visitors, for a drink or a meal.
