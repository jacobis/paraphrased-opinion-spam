The knickbocker is a historical hotel that has gone through some recent renovations to improve its service and attraction. It is more affordable than some other near by hotels while being situated within distance of the same locations and attractions in Chicago. The room tends to be small but comfortable with tasteful decorations and lighting. Bathrooms are clean and well equipped. Hotel rooms are well insulated from sound so you are not disturbed by guests in adjacent rooms. The staff are all around helpful and all had a friendly demeanor.
