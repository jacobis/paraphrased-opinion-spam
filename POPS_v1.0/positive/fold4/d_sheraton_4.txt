The highly-rated Sheraton hotel is renowned for its commitment to excellence from check-in to check-out. Surrounded by immaculate views of the Chicago River and Lake Michigan, guests are pampered from the liveliness and warmth of the hotel staff as well as room accommodations which include the Sheraton's unique "sweet sleeper" bed. The location of the hotel allows guests to easily explore Chicago's Navy Pier and the Magnificent Mile shopping area, and for those who wish to stay in, the hotel offers a delicious selection of food at their restaurant.
