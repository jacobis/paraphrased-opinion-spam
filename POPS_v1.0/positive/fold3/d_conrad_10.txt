The Conrad hotel is nicely situated in the center and is close to all kinds of shopping and tourist attractions. The staff of the hotel is professional, helpful and friendly. The hotel features large rooms and king-sized beds that provide refreshing rest to the guests. The rooms are equipped with large flat screen TVs with nice audio systems and there is a lot of electronic device connectivity as well. The turn down service is quick, neat and has some pleasant touches to it (bottled water). The additional facilities such as the gym and the restaurant provide high level of service as well: the gym is well-equipped and the breakfast options at the restaurant are abundant.
