If you should find yourself looking for a hotel in Downtown Chicago for a great rate, you can't really go wrong with the Conrad located in the heart of the city! The Conrad is very close to everything Chicago has to offer from shopping at Nordstroms to shows and wonderful nearby restaurants. The rooms have a very pleasant atmosphere and the beds are very comfortable and luxurious. The room is also provided with a large plasma TV with surround sound speakers to enjoy during your stay. The staff are top-notch and very friendly and helpful. Overall, I think that you'll really love your stay at the Conrad.
