The Omni is one of the best hotels to stay in Chicago. Located on Michigan Ave, this hotel is in one of the most exquisite areas of Chicago, giving you access to some of the best shops and food that the city has to offer. After a day out in the city, customers who went here were extremely impressed with the level of service at the hotel, stating that they often felt they were treated like royalty. You will feel like you are sitting in the lap of luxury once you enter your suite. Each room is extremely spacious, and has a dazzling array of amenities, such as a huge plasma TV in the living room. This is a hotel you do not want to miss out on!
