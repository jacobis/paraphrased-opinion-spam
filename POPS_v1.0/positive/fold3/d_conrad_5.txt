The Conrad hotel is set in the perfect location downtown with incredible views such as Michigan avenue and the Tribune building! It is located in a great area that is close to many major attractions and sites that you will always have something to do. The rooms are clean and impressive with amazing style and very comfortable beds. They have all of the latest luxuries to offer such as a plasma flat screen television, free internet availability and Ipod radios. There is also an amazing breakfast available that is of top notch restaurant quality. This is an unbeatable hotel for the price and a necessity to stay at when visiting.
