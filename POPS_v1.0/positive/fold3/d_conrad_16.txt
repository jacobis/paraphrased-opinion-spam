The location of this hotel could not be better.  With a mall in the hotel itself, plus all the shopping and entertainment on Michigan Ave, boredom is not an option.  The staff and concierge are incredibly gracious and helpful.  The rooms are amazing, too.  They are meticulously maintained, and boast Large plasma TVs and Bose sound systems.  Robes and slippers are also provided for ultimate comfort and relaxation.  Insider's tip: ask for an odd-numbered room to take in the view on Michigan Ave.
