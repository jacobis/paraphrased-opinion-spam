Hyatt Regency is a great hotel in Chicago with a great location and friendly staff.  The hotel is located close to Michigan Avenue, the subway and within a short distance of local restaurants and popular sightseeing areas.  The rooms are very large, clean and well furnished with a flat screen television and iPod dock alarm clock.  The beds are comfortable with great pillows and beautiful bedding.  Although parking may be a bit expensive, the rooms themselves are very reasonable.  This hotel is a great place to stay on your next visit to Chicago.
